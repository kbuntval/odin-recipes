Salsa Verde

Description

This recipe is as easy as it comes. Literally throw a bunch of ingredients into a pressure cooker and in 15 minutes, you have immensely flavorful, perfectly cooked salsa verde.

The only part of this recipe that may get your hands a little dirty is the shredding of the chicken. But hey, that's what kids are for!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 3 tomatillos, diced
  • 1 poblano, diced

Steps

  1. Throw all of your ingredients into a pressure cooker and set the timer for 10 minutes once at pressure
  2. Allow the natural release of the pressure. Once complete, separate the chicken and start to shred it.
  3. Take the rest of the ingredients and blend them together until smooth.
  4. Return the chicken to the salsa verde and reduce if necessary. Voila!
